NO MEANS NO FESTIVAL


There are so many people that have experienced sexual harassment and either experienced one of the following: no one believed them, they did not receive justice for what has happened to them, or are afraid to come forward due to potential threat/retaliation. Spreading awareness about this topic can help others find their voice and educate people on why this is not okay.


This festival allows others to come and share their story with others. It's a great way to educate those that want to learn about these experiences and how they can help to prevent and spread awareness. Come join us starting December 8th, 2023.
